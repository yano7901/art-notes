🎨 Art Notes

Play an 88-key piano and watch colors come to life!

Click a key to start

How it works:

  • Click any piano key to play a note
  • Each note generates a unique color based on its frequency
  • Low notes (A0-C3) = Warm colors (Red, Orange, Yellow)
  • Mid notes (D3-B5) = Neutral colors (Green, Brown)
  • High notes (C6-C8) = Cool colors (Blue, Purple, White)
  • Use keyboard: Z-M for white keys, S,D,G,H,J for black keys
